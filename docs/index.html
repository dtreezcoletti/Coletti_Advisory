The Platform

Coletti OS

An AI-powered litigation case management platform built specifically for the complexity of divorce and family law financial discovery. Designed by forensic accountants, engineered for attorneys.

  • Python-Powered Forensic Engine — Custom algorithms for income reconstruction, dissipation analysis, and multi-account reconciliation.
  • OCR-Enabled Ingestion — Automated document parsing converts PDFs and image-based bank statements into structured, analyzable data.
  • Court-Document-Grade Output — Reports follow evidence exhibit standards with professional formatting, citation, and signature blocks.
  • Excel & PDF Exports — Counsel-ready workbooks with dynamic schedules, summary dashboards, and labeled exhibits.
